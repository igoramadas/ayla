Ayla

What

An open-source personal assistant and home automation software created by Igor Ramadas.

Why

Because I couldn't find any reasonable solution out there. There are a few personal assistants for iOS and Android. A few home automation softwares and platforms like X10, Insteon and ZigBee. A few fitness trackers and services. A few... too many of everything.

Ayla won't replace any of the above but instead it integrates with them.

How

The "brain" is a Node.js home server, coded mainly using CoffeeScript.

Then there's the client, a Windows Phone 8 app. Yes, Windows Phone! I don't like Android too much, and iOS even less.

Who

Who made it? Again, Igor Ramadas. Who can use it? Anyone, including you.
